Semantic Tags

HOT TAKE- HEADERS ARE THE MOST IMPORTANT PART OF THE ARTICLE

Semantics is the study of the meanings of words and phrases in a language
Semantic elemends are tags that are clearly defined.
Semantic tags are for meaning in the web page rather than just for presentation.
Used to make the webpage more readable for users/developers.
Provides additional information about that document, like why a certain block of text is there

Examples of semantic tags

  • form- defines a form for users to collect input
    first name:
    last name:
  • table- defines a table
    :D :) :(
    :P xD :3
  • figure- can hold an image as well as a figure caption

Things that are not Semantic Tags

div, span
div and span tell us nothing about what goes into them.
While they can fulfill the same functionality as an article tag,
we as users know that the content in an article tag is relevant to
blogposts or newspaper articles.
